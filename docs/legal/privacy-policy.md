### Scope

LIKWID is a decentralized finance protocol (the “Protocol”) that operates in a non-custodial and permissionless manner, enabling users to interact with smart contracts directly via their public wallet addresses. This Privacy Policy covers only information collected via the **Site** and **interface** (if any) operated by the LIKWID team. It does **not** apply to interactions with third-party frontends, interfaces, or wallets.
